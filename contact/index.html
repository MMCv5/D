Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How long does it take to hear back from AIIA?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our direct line.

What types of partnerships does AIIA facilitate?

We facilitate strategic project partnerships, technology licensing, joint ventures, talent exchanges, and soft landing programs.

Do you work with early-stage companies?

Yes, we work with companies at all stages, from early-stage startups to large corporations, provided they align with our focus sectors.

Is there a fee for initial consultations?

Initial consultations and partnership discussions are complimentary. We'll discuss any fees upfront based on the scope of services required.

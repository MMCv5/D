Partnership Models in Detail

Strategic Project Partnerships

Large-scale collaborative ventures

AIIA builds the right consortium of Australian partners, bringing together complementary expertise, resources, and capabilities to deliver complex, high-impact projects.

Key Benefits

  • Access to pre-vetted Australian consortium partners
  • Reduced project risk through diversified expertise
  • Streamlined stakeholder engagement and coordination
  • Government relations and regulatory support
  • End-to-end project management capabilities

Our Process

  1. 1Project scoping and requirements analysis
  2. 2Partner identification and due diligence
  3. 3Consortium formation and governance structure
  4. 4Stakeholder alignment and agreement execution
  5. 5Ongoing project coordination and support
Timeline: 6-18 monthsInvestment: High ($10M+)

Technology Scouting & Licensing

Access to cutting-edge Australian IP

AIIA acts as your trusted, on-the-ground partner with deep networks across universities, research institutions, and innovative companies to identify and evaluate high-potential technologies.

Key Benefits

  • Comprehensive technology landscape mapping
  • Direct access to university technology transfer offices
  • IP due diligence and valuation support
  • Negotiation facilitation and deal structuring
  • Ongoing relationship management with IP holders

Our Process

  1. 1Technology requirements specification
  2. 2Systematic market and research scanning
  3. 3Technology evaluation and ranking
  4. 4Introduction to IP holders and initial discussions
  5. 5Deal facilitation and agreement execution
Timeline: 3-12 monthsInvestment: Medium ($1M-$10M)

Joint Venture (JV) Facilitation

Creating new commercial entities

AIIA matches compatible partners and provides comprehensive support throughout the JV formation process, from initial partner identification to operational setup and governance.

Key Benefits

  • Strategic partner matching and compatibility assessment
  • JV structure design and legal framework setup
  • Governance model development and board composition
  • Operational integration and cultural alignment support
  • Ongoing relationship management and conflict resolution

Our Process

  1. 1Partner profiling and strategic fit analysis
  2. 2Initial partner introductions and relationship building
  3. 3JV structure negotiation and agreement drafting
  4. 4Legal entity formation and regulatory compliance
  5. 5Operational launch and ongoing support
Timeline: 9-18 monthsInvestment: High ($5M+)

Talent & Expert Exchange Programs

Two-way knowledge and skills transfer

These programs create lasting connections between organizations while building mutual understanding, transferring knowledge, and developing cross-cultural capabilities that drive long-term success.

Key Benefits

  • Access to world-class Australian talent and expertise
  • Cultural immersion and cross-cultural competency development
  • Knowledge transfer and capability building
  • Network expansion and relationship development
  • Reduced recruitment and training costs

Our Process

  1. 1Program design and objectives setting
  2. 2Participant selection and matching
  3. 3Exchange logistics and support arrangement
  4. 4Monitoring and mentorship throughout exchange
  5. 5Knowledge capture and program evaluation
Timeline: 6-24 monthsInvestment: Low-Medium ($100K-$1M)

Australian Market 'Soft Landing'

Structured market entry support

This program provides end-to-end support for market entry, from initial market research and regulatory guidance to operational setup and local partnership development.

Key Benefits

  • Comprehensive market research and opportunity assessment
  • Regulatory compliance and legal structure guidance
  • Local office setup and operational support
  • Network introductions and partnership facilitation
  • Ongoing business development and growth support

Our Process

  1. 1Market entry strategy development
  2. 2Regulatory and legal framework navigation
  3. 3Local presence establishment (office, staff, systems)
  4. 4Market entry execution and initial operations
  5. 5Growth acceleration and partnership development
Timeline: 12-24 monthsInvestment: Medium-High ($2M-$10M)
